# [Zapier Integration](https://www.smarty.com/docs/plugins/zapier-plugin)
All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

## 1.0.6 - 2026-06-23

ORIGINAL RELEASE:
- The Smarty Address Verification integration for Zapier validates, verifies, and enriches US and international addresses inside any Zap.
- Validate a single US street address.
- Validate up to 100 US street addresses in a single batch step.
- Verify international street addresses across 240+ countries.
- Enrich a US address with property, geo-reference, secondary, or business data.
- Connect a Smarty account with OAuth 2.0 — no API keys to copy or paste.
- Choose a secret key and license per step, with a warning when the connected account is missing a required subscription.
